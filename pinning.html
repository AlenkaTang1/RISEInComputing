Celebrating Excellence in Computing

The RISE Pinning Ceremony is a cherished tradition that honors the achievements, dedication, and excellence of our computing students at Georgia State University.

The Tradition

The pinning ceremony symbolizes our students' commitment to excellence in computing and their connection to the RISE community. Each pin represents their dedication to advancing diversity and inclusion in computer science.

The Celebration

During this special event, students receive their pins in the presence of faculty, family, and friends. It's a moment to recognize their hard work and celebrate their journey in computing.

The Community

The ceremony brings together the entire RISE community to celebrate our shared commitment to excellence and diversity in computing education at Georgia State University.
