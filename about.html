About Our Program

RISE In Computing at GSU is dedicated to fostering an inclusive environment in computer science education, with a particular focus on supporting and empowering Black women in computing. Our program combines academic excellence with community building to create lasting change in the field of computer science.

Dr. Anu Bourgeois

Co-founder
