Mission of RISE

Reaching for Inclusion – Striving for Excellence (RISE) in Computing at GSU is a community to support undergraduate computing students with an emphasis on enhancing the lived experiences of Black women in computing, with aims to increase retention, persistence, and graduation of Black women, develop a more welcoming departmental culture for Black women in computing at GSU and provide better social mobility, career options, & job security for Black women post-graduation.
